## Slide 1: Fixing Capital Mispricing
**[Screen: `create-room.html` / `company-verify.html`]**

"The biggest problem for companies like DRG is **Capital Mispricing**. Right now, a good project ends up paying for the mistakes of unrelated business units. 

In Slide 1, we fix this. Instead of evaluating the entire company as one risky entity, we use this **Initiation Wizard** to isolate the Solar project into a structured, independent deal. We verify the company's identity first, then pack the specific project's cash flow separately.

**The result?** This allows DRG to be priced based on project fundamentals, not legacy risk—leading to more accurate risk pricing."

---

## Slide 2: Fixing Information Asymmetry
**[Screen: `deal-discovery.html` / `permission.html`]**

"The second friction is **Information Asymmetry**. DRG has good data, but they often fear that sharing too much too early will leak sensitive trade secrets. Because they don't share, Lenders don't trust. 

We solve this using a **Tiered Access Mechanism**, which reduces the information gap between borrower and lender. On the Marketplace, Lenders only see a 'Blind Profile' with basic IRR and NPV, but not customer contracts or supplier details until access is granted. If they are interested, they must request access. 

As the relationship grows, DRG can progressively reveal more data through our **Permissions Module**. This means DRG doesn't have to choose between secrecy and transparency—the system allows controlled disclosure, where trust is built progressively."

---

## Slide 3: Fixing Slow & Costly Due Diligence
**[Screen: `deal-room.html` / `upload-docs.html`]**

"In the real world, Due Diligence (DD) is a nightmare. It takes 3 to 4 months of back-and-forth emails, lost files, and repeated requests. 

We fix this with our **Standardized Data Room**. Everything the Lender needs—Financials, Legal, ESG—is already organized into 4 core folders. 

We also implemented a 'Security Overlay'. Every time a Lender views a file, they see a dynamic watermark and we track their activity. Because everything is centralized and traceable, we eliminate the friction of manual auditing. We're not just moving files; we're potentially reducing the deal cycle from 3-4 months to just a few weeks, while also lowering the cost of due diligence."

---

## Slide 4: Fixing the Trust Gap (Core Tech)
**[Screen: `blockchain-monitor.html` / `blockchain-ledger.html`]**

"Finally, we address the **Trust Gap**. Even with a Data Room, Lenders still worry: *'Is this PDF tampered with?'* or *'Did they swap the financial report last night?'*

This is where our **Blockchain Integrity Monitor** comes in. We don't use blockchain everywhere—only where data integrity is critical. Every core document uploaded by DRG is 'hashed' and anchored to the blockchain instantly. 

During the demo, if anyone tries to override a file with fake data, our monitor will catch the mismatch and trigger a **Red Alert** immediately. And more importantly, this is not just a warning—the system can flag the deal as compromised in real time. 

Instead of relying on trust or manual audit, the system enforces data integrity—reducing the need for manual audit and directly lowering the risk premium applied by lenders."
